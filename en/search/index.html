WE Algerians EdCamp

In response to the COVID-19 crisis in Algeria and its direct impact on the educational system, We Algerians is organizing an Education week camp We Algerians EdCamp that unfolds two objectives:

(1) to introduce instructors and students to the world of online education and the flexibility it can offer during this pandemic;

(2) to provide a think tank to brainstorm about the future of the Algerian higher-education institutions and how they can be more competitive.
