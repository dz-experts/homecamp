WE Algerians EdCamp

In response to the COVID-19 crisis in Algeria and its direct impact on the educational system, WE Algerians is organizing an Education week camp WE Algerians EdCamp that meets two objectives:

  • introduce teachers and students to online learning and training a convenient and flexible key solution during COVID-19 pandemic;

  • provide a Think Tank to enable brainstorming about the future of Algerian education institutions, and how they can be more competitive.
